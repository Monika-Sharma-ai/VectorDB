Where Does Approximation Happen?

Trace the query through the index — every approximation has a cost and a benefit.

Three places approximation enters the pipeline:

  • ① Cell selection — only nprobe closest cells are scanned (miss if NN is elsewhere)
  • ② Distance estimate — PQ codes give approximate distances
  • ③ Beam search — HNSW explores a bounded neighborhood, not the full graph

Re-ranking with full float vectors on top-100 candidates recovers most of the lost recall — for very little extra latency.
