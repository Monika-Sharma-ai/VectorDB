Key takeaways

  • Exact NN is impossible at scale — trade recall for speed
  • HNSW is today's gold standard (default for <100M)
  • IVF + PQ wins when memory matters (1B+ scale)
  • Real systems combine: HNSW + IVF + PQ + re-rank
